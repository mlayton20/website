The practice was established in 1997 building up a reputation as an efficient and professional practice.

Introducing you to the team involved in your care

All our dental care professionals adhere to the GDC Standards for Dental Professionals and are registered with the General Dental Council (GDC).

www.gdc-uk.org

Your Dentists

Christopher Thomas - GDC Number: 53870

Chris qualified at Guys Hospital London in 1979, after which he secured a position as a House Surgeon at Guys, gaining valuable post-qualification experience. Following his time in London, Chris worked in Bristol and Bolsover and subsequently settled in Derby where he purchased his first practice in 1983. In 1997, Etwall Dental Practice opened following the conversion into the practice you see today.

Rebekah Hyde - GDC Number: 65816

Rebekah qualified from Bristol Dental Hospital in 1990. Rebekah came to work for Chris at the Derby practice in 1991 and has worked in the derby area since then, joining Etwall Dental Practice in 2007.

Farah Younis - GDC Number: 85559

Farah qualified at The university of Birmingham in 2005. She completed her vocational training in Borrowash and continued on to work as a SHO at the DRI. Following completion of the MFDS qualification from the Royal college of surgeons of Edinburgh. She continued to gain experience in the community, NHS and private dental sectors. She joined Etwall Dental Practice Derby in March 2009.

Mandhir Bains - GDC Number: 193725

Mandhir qualified at Barts & The London in 2010. He studied his secondary and 6th form studies locally at John Port School in Etwall village, Derby. He is an ethical and conscientious dentist, with a wide range of clinical experience, in both NHS and private treatments. Mandhir has great enthusiasm in continual professional development and further education.

Jaspreet Matharu - GDC Number: 202884

Jaspreet qualified as a Dentist from Barts & The London in 2010. She completed her dental foundation training at a dental practice in Kidderminster and gained further experience as a Senior House officer at QMC Maxillofacial department. Since then has worked in both hospital and dental practice gaining experience in both NHS and private dentistry. She is a caring and conscientious dentist who has completed her MUDF exams and will shortly complete her certificate in Dental education. In her spare time she enjoys keeping fit and cooking.
