If you require emergency treatment please telephone the practice early in the morning and we will endeavour to see you as soon as possible, usually the same day.

For out of hours emergencies please telephone the practice and follow the advice given.

If you do have to cancel an appointment please give the practice at least 24 hours notice so that we can try to offer the appointment to another patient.

This will avoid a cancellation or missed appointment charge.
