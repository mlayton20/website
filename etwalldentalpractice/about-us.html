The practice was established in 1997 building up a reputation as an efficient and professional practice.

Introducing you to the team involved in your care

All our dental care professionals adhere to the GDC Standards for Dental Professionals and are registered with the General Dental Council (GDC).

Your Dentists

Christopher Thomas - GDC Number: 53870

Chris qualified at Guys Hospital London in 1979, after which he secured a position as a House Surgeon at Guys, gaining valuable post-qualification experience. Following his time in London, Chris worked in Bristol and Bolsover and subsequently settled in Derby where he purchased his first practice in 1983. In 1997, Etwall Dental Practice opened following the conversion into the practice you see today.

Kunal Patel - GDC Number: TBC

I qualified in 2010 from The University of Sheffield. I enjoy carrying out all aspects of Dentistry, and my passion and area of expertise is adult orthodontics and cosmetic dentistry. Since qualifying I have attended many post-graduate courses worldwide to further my knowledge in the dental field. I have sat and passed post graduate royal college of surgeon exams and am continually studying, attending courses and pushing myself to further my knowledge in the dental field.

For me, being a dentist is all about getting to know my patients and understanding their dental concerns – it's not just about performing the treatment itself. I care about what I do, and endeavour to work to the best of my abilities in providing service and quality dental care to my patients.

I have completed:

  • 12 month certificate course with Chris Orr in advanced cosmetic dentistry
  • Yorkshire Sedation training

I am certified provider of:

  • Six Month Smile
  • Smilelign
  • Inman aligner
  • IAS Clear Brace
  • Enlighten teeth whitening

As well as these certification courses, I have attended numerous one or two day shorter courses and lectures around the UK.

If I'm not behind a dental chair, you will find me either trying to go to the gym or in the driving range trying to make it on the golf course!

Jaspreet Matharu - GDC Number: 202884

Asha Chhaya - GDC Number: 211399

Asha graduated from Kings College London in 2011 with an Honours degree. Her determination to excel enabled her to achieve many awards during her time at dental school.
